Press A Key to Start

Game Rules

X
  • Press Any key to start.
  • The game begins with a random sequence of colors and tones played by the computer.
  • The player must replicate the sequence by clicking on the corresponding colored buttons.
  • After each successful repetition of the sequence, the computer adds an additional color and tone to the sequence, making it longer and more challenging.
  • The game continues until the player makes a mistake or reaches a certain level.
